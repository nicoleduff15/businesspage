About Me

My name is Alexandra Flores I've been obsessed with beauty for as long as I can remember. My passion for hair, make-up, and styling started when I was in high school. I would first experiment and teach myself how to do make-up and even cut and style my own hair. I was a walking billboard for myself. It quickly got noticed and before I knew it I was booked the day of prom making the all the girls feel glamorous. My closet was constantly being shared with my best friends. I loved helping them pick the perfect outfit!

I knew I wanted this to be my career and received my cosmetology license right after high school and never looked back. I was fortuante enough to gain experience in Los Angeles, New York, and now working in San Francisco. Being an infinite learner is what sets me apart from most. I strive to be the best stylist/artist possible and make my clients look and feel their best. When you look good you feel good!

I appreciate you coming to my page and viewing my work. I look forward to working with you soon!
